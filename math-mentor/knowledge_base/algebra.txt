=== QUADRATIC EQUATIONS ===
Standard form: ax² + bx + c = 0
Quadratic formula: x = (-b ± √(b²-4ac)) / 2a
Discriminant: D = b² - 4ac
- D > 0: two distinct real roots
- D = 0: two equal real roots
- D < 0: no real roots (complex roots)

Sum of roots: α + β = -b/a
Product of roots: αβ = c/a

Nature of roots:
- If D is a perfect square → rational roots
- If D > 0 and not perfect square → irrational roots

=== POLYNOMIALS ===
Factor theorem: (x-a) is a factor of p(x) if p(a) = 0
Remainder theorem: When p(x) is divided by (x-a), remainder = p(a)

Vieta's formulas for cubic ax³+bx²+cx+d=0:
- Sum of roots: α+β+γ = -b/a
- Sum of products two at a time: αβ+βγ+γα = c/a
- Product of roots: αβγ = -d/a

=== SEQUENCES AND SERIES ===
Arithmetic Progression (AP):
- General term: aₙ = a + (n-1)d
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 × [2a + (n-1)d]
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = n/2 × (a + l) where l = last term

Geometric Progression (GP):
- General term: aₙ = ar^(n-1)
- Sum of n terms: Sₙ = a(rⁿ-1)/(r-1) for r≠1
- Sum of infinite GP: S∞ = a/(1-r) for |r| < 1

Harmonic Progression (HP):
- General term: 1/aₙ is in AP
- HM of a and b: H = 2ab/(a+b)

AM-GM-HM inequality: AM ≥ GM ≥ HM

=== INEQUALITIES ===
AM-GM: (a+b)/2 ≥ √(ab) for positive a,b
Cauchy-Schwarz: (a²+b²)(c²+d²) ≥ (ac+bd)²

=== BINOMIAL THEOREM ===
(a+b)ⁿ = Σ C(n,r) × aⁿ⁻ʳ × bʳ, r from 0 to n
General term: Tᵣ₊₁ = C(n,r) × aⁿ⁻ʳ × bʳ
Middle term: If n is even, middle term = T_(n/2+1)

=== LOGARITHMS ===
logₐ(mn) = logₐm + logₐn
logₐ(m/n) = logₐm - logₐn
logₐ(mⁿ) = n × logₐm
Change of base: logₐb = log b / log a
logₐa = 1, logₐ1 = 0

=== COMPLEX NUMBERS ===
i = √(-1), i² = -1, i³ = -i, i⁴ = 1
|z| = √(a²+b²) for z = a+bi
Argument: θ = arctan(b/a)
De Moivre: (cosθ + i sinθ)ⁿ = cos(nθ) + i sin(nθ)
Euler: e^(iθ) = cosθ + i sinθ

=== COMMON MISTAKES IN ALGEBRA ===
- Forgetting ± when taking square roots
- Sign errors when expanding (a-b)²  = a² - 2ab + b²  NOT a² + b²
- (a+b)² ≠ a² + b²  (must include 2ab)
- Division by zero when variable is in denominator
- log of negative numbers is undefined in real domain
